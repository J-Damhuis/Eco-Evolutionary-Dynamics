This folder contains four items:

van velzen & etienne - evolution of defense under ecological costs.doc is the manuscript version submitted (and accepted with minor revisions) to Journal of Theoretical Biology,
"The importance of ecological costs for the evolution of plant defense against herbivory", Ellen van Velzen & Rampal Etienne

"simulation code" contains the c++ code for the different model versions.

"maple files" contains the maple files with the adaptive dynamics analysis (symbolic and numerical).

"sigmaplot files" contains the sigmaplot files with the data and graphs for the final figures
